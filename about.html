Who We Are

A Legacy of Innovation,
A Future Without Limits

JBG Global Solutions is one of the fastest-growing multi-domain service organizations, delivering integrated solutions across technology, automation, digital marketing, staffing, and infrastructure to clients worldwide.

Founded with the belief that technology should simplify business — not complicate it — JBG Global Solutions began its journey in Hyderabad, India, with a clear mission: to make enterprise-grade IT capabilities accessible to organizations of every size. What started as a focused IT consulting practice has evolved into a comprehensive global delivery powerhouse.

Today, with a robust global delivery model spanning onshore and offshore locations, over 500 skilled professionals, and a proven track record of 200+ successful engagements, we serve as a strategic partner to Fortune 500 companies, high-growth startups, and government organizations across 12+ countries. Our strength lies in combining deep domain expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver measurable business outcomes.

From AI-powered automation and cloud-native platforms to full-stack digital transformation and infrastructure modernization, JBG Global Solutions is your single-window partner for accelerating innovation and achieving operational excellence at scale.

Our Story

The JBG Global Journey

From a focused IT consulting practice to a global multi-domain powerhouse — a timeline of relentless growth.

2010

The Beginning

Founded in Hyderabad with a clear focus on IT consulting and custom software development, serving local enterprises with world-class engineering talent.

2013

Expansion into Digital & BPO

Broadened service portfolio to include Digital Marketing, Business Process Outsourcing, and IT staffing — tripling revenue and client base within 18 months.

2016

AI/ML & RPA Center of Excellence

Launched dedicated AI/ML practice and RPA Center of Excellence, becoming early adopters of intelligent automation for enterprise workflows.

2019

Global Delivery Centers

Opened global delivery centers across multiple geographies, expanded team to 100+ employees, and secured partnerships with Fortune 500 clients.

2022

Infrastructure & Premium Consulting

Launched Infrastructure Development division and Premium Consulting practice, enabling end-to-end enterprise transformation for large-scale clients.

2025

Scaling New Heights

Achieved landmark milestones: 500+ professionals, 50+ global clients, presence across 12+ countries, and recognition as a top emerging IT services firm.

2026

The Future Is Now

Expanding with Quantum AI Lab, Innovation Hubs, and next-generation product lines — positioning JBG Global Solutions as a leader in the next wave of digital enterprise transformation.
